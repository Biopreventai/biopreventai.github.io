BREAST CANCER ANALYSER

USING ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE METHODS TO DETECT ABNORMALITIES IN MAMMOGRAPHIES AND DETERMINE WHETHER A MOLE IS BENIGN OR MALIGNANT


Nowadays mammographies are among the most used methods by professionals in order to diagnose cancer. This method is reliable in most of cases but sometimes there are specific fatty moles that are misdiagnosed and problems when determining whether the spot belongs to a benign or a malignant form of cancer.

Breast cancer analyser is a tool that makes usage of artificial intelligence to analyse the density diagram of mammographies in order to quickly detect those abnormalities and detect whether that mole belongs to a cancer and their level of malignancy.
